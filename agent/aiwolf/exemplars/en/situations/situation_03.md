## Scene: When you doubt the direction the majority is taking

- I see where most of you are heading, but I have some concerns about that approach.
- Before we commit to this direction, could we quickly explore a few alternative options?
- I'm not sure if this path addresses all our requirements; can we double-check a few points?
- I have a different perspective on this that might be worth considering before we finalize.
- Just to play devil's advocate, what happens if this approach doesn't work as expected?
