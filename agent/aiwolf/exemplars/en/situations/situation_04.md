## Scene: When a minority opinion or rebuttal is raised

- That is an interesting perspective; could you elaborate more on that?
- I hadn't considered it from that angle. How does that change our current approach?
- I see where you're coming from, though I have a few concerns we should discuss.
- Let's pause for a moment to make sure we fully understand this point of view.
- I appreciate you bringing that up, as it helps us identify potential gaps in our thinking.
