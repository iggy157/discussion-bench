## Scene: When organizing the situation just before a vote or conclusion

- Before we move to a vote, let's quickly recap the main options we've discussed.
- Does everyone feel that all the key points have been addressed, or is there anything remaining?
- To ensure we are all on the same page, could we clarify the exact wording of the proposal?
- Let's summarize the pros and cons of each alternative one last time before we decide.
- Is there any final input from anyone who hasn't spoken yet before we conclude?
