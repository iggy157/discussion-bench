## Scene: When you sense a contradiction in someone's claim

- I'm a bit confused; how does that align with what you mentioned earlier?
- I might have misunderstood, but those two points seem to contradict each other.
- Could you help me reconcile that with the previous statement?
- I noticed a slight discrepancy between these two points; could you clarify?
- If that is the case, how does it fit with the other point you made?
