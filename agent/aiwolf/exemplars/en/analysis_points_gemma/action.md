# Analysis — action (points, from scripts_k5)

### Good Discussion Checklist: Strategic Actions

**Day 0: Establishing the Framework**
* **Set Procedural Norms:** Agree on a "reveal early" policy for the Seer to prevent information hoarding.
* **Define Evaluation Criteria:** Establish that claims will be judged on reasoning and behavioral consistency, not volume or confidence.
* **Baseline Behavioral Mapping:** Use the lack of data to identify player archetypes (e.g., the "pusher," the "cautious," the "observer") to detect later deviations.

**Day 1: Resolving the Seer Conflict**
* **Single Claim (1-CO):**
    * Validate the target choice: Does the Seer's reasoning for picking that specific player align with Day 0 behavior?
    * Cross-reference the result with the target's reactions to see if they are "too" defensive or suspiciously quiet.
* **Double Claim (2-CO):**
    * **Analyze Timing:** Identify the "second mover." A claim that mirrors the first Seer's target is a high-probability "Possessed" cover.
    * **Test for Verifiability:** Distinguish between the Seer who offers to be tested by a second night's result vs. the one rushing an immediate execution to bury the evidence.
    * **Identify "Cover Pairs":** Look for players who immediately defend a specific Seer claim without evidence, suggesting a Wolf-Possessed alliance.

**Day 2+: Post-Execution Analysis**
* **The "Flip" Pivot:** Immediately use the role reveal of the executed player to validate the remaining Seer's honesty.
* **Audit Voting History:** Trace who defended the fake Seer or pushed to execute the real one; these players are the primary suspects for the remaining Wolf.
* **Eliminate the "Cleared":** Treat players confirmed as "Human" by a validated Seer as the trusted core to corner the remaining Wolf.

**General 5-Player Dynamics**
* **Budgeted Utterances:** Keep turns concise; avoid filler. Every move must either provide a read, challenge a logic gap, or signal alignment.
* **Avoid Tunneling:** Acknowledge hunches but prioritize "hard" data (divine results) and "structural" data (voting patterns) over "soft" data (vibes/tone).
