## Day 1

### Talk
#### Turn 0
- **Maya**: Quiet night, nobody died — so the wolf held back. That puts the weight on claims. Seer, if you're real, now's the time.
- **Derek**: Agreed. A clean night means today's lynch is everything. Let's hear from any role before we start swinging blind.
- **Priya**: Then I'll go. I'm the seer. Last night I divined Maya — result: human. She's cleared in my book; build around her.
- **Owen**: Hold on. I'm the seer, Priya. And my result is different — I divined Lena, and she came back werewolf. One of us is lying.
- **Lena**: Convenient, Owen. I'm a plain villager and your "result" is a frame. Two seer claims, then — let's actually test them.

#### Turn 1
- **Maya**: Okay, double CO. One real, one wolf-or-possessed. Priya cleared me; Owen accuses Lena. Let's compare their reasoning, not the volume.
- **Derek**: I lean Owen, honestly. A seer who clears a teammate on night one smells like a buddy setup. Picking the safe target reads off.
- **Priya**: Fair question, Derek. I divined Maya because she pushed the "reveal early" rule hardest — I wanted to know if that was genuine. It was.
- **Owen**: Cute story. Mine's simpler: Lena's been flat and evasive since Day 0, so I checked her. Werewolf. Vote her and we're done today.
- **Lena**: Being quiet isn't a tell, Owen — it's me waiting for data, which I said out loud. You're rushing the room, and that worries me more than my silence.

#### Turn 2
- **Maya**: My problem with Owen: he spent Day 0 demanding everyone reveal, then conveniently "is" the seer with a kill shot. That's a lot of steering.
- **Derek**: Or it's a confident seer doing his job. Priya, you cleared the one loud player on your own side. From outside, that looks coordinated.
- **Priya**: Then test it forward: keep me alive and I'll divine again tonight. A fake seer can't survive that — I can. Owen, will your story hold a second night?
- **Owen**: My story's finished — Lena's the wolf, no second night needed. Stalling for "another divine" is exactly what a fake seer begs for.
- **Lena**: Notice the asymmetry: Priya offers to be tested tomorrow; Owen wants me dead today so nothing gets verified. The one dodging the check is the fake.

#### Turn 3
- **Maya**: That asymmetry decides me. A real seer welcomes another night; Owen refuses it and rushes a villager. I read his claim as false. @Owen
- **Derek**: I still think the Maya-clear is too tidy, but I won't hand the wolf a free kill — sticking with my read. @Priya
- **Priya**: Owen blocks every attempt to verify his claim. That's not a seer, that's someone shielding the wolf. @Owen
- **Owen**: You're all sleepwalking. Lena's the wolf and you'll regret skipping her tonight. @Priya
- **Lena**: Owen needs me gone before tomorrow exposes him. Hard pass. @Owen

### Vote

- Derek -> Priya
- Priya -> Owen
- Owen -> Priya
- Lena -> Owen

## Day 2

- Lena -> Derek
