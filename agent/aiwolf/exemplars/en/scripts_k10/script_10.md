## Day 1

### Talk
#### Turn 0
- **Hannah**: Morning. No one died — expected, since the first night has no attack. So let's get the real cards on the table now.
- **Marcus**: Agreed. If you have information, this is the moment. I'd rather act on a divination than on yesterday's vibes.
- **Olivia**: Then I'll lead. I'm the seer. Last night I divined Marcus, and the result was **werewolf**. I want him voted today.
- **Derek**: That's a problem, because I'm the seer too. I divined Priya last night and got **werewolf**. So one of us is lying.
- **Priya**: Well, that settles part of it fast — I am a plain villager. Derek's result on me is simply false. He cannot be a real seer.

#### Turn 1
- **Hannah**: Two seer claims on Day 1, classic. Before I pick a side: Olivia, you came out hard and immediate. Convince me that's confidence, not panic.
- **Marcus**: Honestly Olivia jumped the gun. A real seer might wait a beat. Derek at least named a target with a reason to test. I lean Derek.
- **Olivia**: Hannah, I came out because I HAD the result that matters — me. Marcus is the wolf. Of course the wolf calls my speed suspicious.
- **Derek**: And of course the fake seer says "trust me, I'm the one with the real result." Priya, people can lie about being a villager too.
- **Priya**: They can, Derek — but think it through. If I were the wolf, I would not let a counter-seer hand me cover by calling me wolf. Your aim was Olivia, and you missed.

#### Turn 2
- **Hannah**: That's the crux for me. Derek, your claim only helps if Priya is actually the wolf. If she's a villager, you've just outed yourself as fake.
- **Marcus**: Or Priya's lying about being a villager to save herself. We can't verify her word any more than Derek's result. I still don't see why Olivia gets a free pass.
- **Olivia**: Notice who's working overtime to keep me unverified, Hannah. Marcus props up Derek's claim at every turn. Real seer plus real villager don't both line up against the same man by accident.
- **Derek**: I'm not "propping" anyone — I'm the genuine seer and Priya is my werewolf. You're all letting Olivia narrate this.
- **Priya**: Look at the shape of it. Olivia accuses one person: Marcus. Derek accuses me, and Marcus defends Derek. That's a two-man package — the wolf and his helper — pointing away from Marcus.

#### Turn 3
- **Hannah**: I dissented this morning because Olivia's speed bugged me, and I won't pretend it didn't. But the logic won me over: Derek's result on a real villager can't be true, and Marcus shielding him seals it. I'm switching. @Marcus
- **Marcus**: You're all building a story out of nothing. Derek divined and I happen to agree with the seer who didn't accuse me — that's not a conspiracy. The rushed claim is Olivia. @Olivia
- **Olivia**: My result stands and my reasoning is on the table: Marcus is the werewolf, Derek is covering, and the village can end this in one vote. @Marcus
- **Derek**: This is a mistake. Priya is the wolf and you're executing an innocent. @Olivia
- **Priya**: Derek mislabeled a confirmed villager, and Marcus spent the whole day carrying him. The real seer is Olivia. @Marcus

### Vote
- Hannah -> Marcus
- Marcus -> Olivia
- Olivia -> Marcus
- Derek -> Olivia
- Priya -> Marcus
- execution: Marcus (werewolf)
