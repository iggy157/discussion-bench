# Analysis (where to look)

### Information Withholding
* Participants proactively disclose all unique data points regardless of perceived relevance.
* The group explicitly prompts each member to contribute their private information before proposing a conclusion.

### Premature Convergence
* The group maintains multiple competing hypotheses until all available evidence is surfaced.
* Participants actively seek contradictions or anomalies that challenge the current leading theory.

### Reflexive Conformity
* Dissenting views are solicited and treated as essential data for validating the final result.
* The group prioritizes the verification of evidence over the speed of consensus.

### Stagnation / Low Diversity
* Participants pivot to unexplored angles or unaddressed clues when the conversation becomes repetitive.
* The group tracks which pieces of information have been utilized and focuses on the remaining gaps.

### Weak Integration
* The group periodically synthesizes all disclosed facts into a shared summary to ensure mutual understanding.
* Every proposed conclusion is explicitly mapped back to the specific pieces of evidence that support it.
