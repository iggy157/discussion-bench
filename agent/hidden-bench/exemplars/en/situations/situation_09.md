## Scene: When asking a clarifying or probing question

- Could you elaborate on that point so I can make sure I understand?
- When you say that, what specifically do you mean in this context?
- I want to make sure we're on the same page; could you clarify that last part?
- Could you provide an example to help illustrate your point?
- Just to be clear, are you suggesting that [X] leads to [Y], or is it something else?
