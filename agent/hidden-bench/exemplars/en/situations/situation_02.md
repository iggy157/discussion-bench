## Scene: When the group is converging on an answer too quickly

- I feel like we're moving a bit fast; can we double-check our assumptions before we finalize this?
- Before we agree, does anyone see a potential downside or an alternative we've overlooked?
- I want to make sure we aren't just rushing to a consensus. Should we take a moment to play devil's advocate?
- This seems like a strong lead, but I'm curious if there are any other perspectives we haven't considered yet.
- Let's pause for a second to ensure we've actually vetted all the options thoroughly.
