## Scene: When you disagree with the majority

- I see where everyone is coming from, but I have a different perspective I'd like to share.
- I understand the general consensus, but I'm concerned about a few points we might be overlooking.
- I respectfully disagree with the current direction; could we explore an alternative approach?
- While that seems to be the majority view, I think there's another angle we should consider.
- I'm not quite convinced yet, so would it be alright if I play devil's advocate for a moment?
